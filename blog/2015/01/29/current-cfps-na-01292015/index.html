Conference Calls for Proposals in North America (01/29)

| Comments

With a slow start on many conferences opening up their calls for proposals this year, many have begun opening up for new speaker proposals. Here’s a list of many of the interesting conference CFPs in North America at the moment, sorted by CFP close date:

  • POSSCON (Columbia SC, April 14-15): CFP closes January 30th
  • Dev objective (Bloomington MN, May 12-15): CFP closes January 31st
  • IoT Stream Conf (San Francisco, April 28): CFP closes January 31st
  • Mountain West (Salt Lake City, March 3-4) CFP closes January 31st
  • Monitorama (Portland, June 15-17): CFP closes Feb 1st
  • OSCON: CFP closes February 2nd
  • Velocity: CFP closes February 2nd
  • Twilio Signal (San Francisco, May 19-20): CFP closes February 20th
  • LambdaConf (Boulder CO, May 22-24): CFP closes February 27th
  • Open Source Bridge (Portland, June 23-26): CFP closes March 7th
  • Droidcon Montreal (Montreal QC, April 9-10): CFP closes March 8th
  • Front Porch (Dallas, Oct 7): CFP closes March 20th
  • GopherCon (Denver, July 7-10): CFP closes March 31st
  • QCon (New York, June 8-12): CFP closes April 8th
  • Internet of Things (Boston, May 28-29): CFP closes April 23rd
  • Future Insights Live (Las Vegas, June 1-4)
  • Internet of Things Summit (San Francisco, November 19-20): CFP closes October 15th
  • NodeConf (Oakland CA, June 8-9)
