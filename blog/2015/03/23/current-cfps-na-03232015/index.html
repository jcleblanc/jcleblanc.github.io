Conference Calls for Proposals in North America (03/23)

| Comments

Hi all,

We have a nice list of good conference calls for proposals out right now in North America. I’ve also included OSCON out in Amsterdam to the list, because it’s a heavy hitter conference. Enjoy.

  • Pittsburgh Techfest (Pittsburg, June 13)
  • Forward JS (San Francisco, July)
  • AppNation IoT (Santa Clara, July 21): CFP closes June 1st
  • Code on the Beach (Atlantic City, August 7-8): CFP closes March 31st
  • That Conference (Wisconsin Dells, August 10-12): CFP opens March 30th
  • 360|iDev (Denver, August 16-19)
  • Northeast PHP (Boston, August 22-23)
  • Surge (National Harbor MD, September 23-25): CFP closes March 31st
  • DataWeek (Santa Clara, Sept 28-30): CFP closes August 7th
  • All Things Open (Raleigh, Oct 18-20)
  • HTML5DevConf (San Francisco, Oct 19-23): CFP closes June 24th
  • CSS Dev Conf (Long Beach, October 26-28): CFP closes April 5th
  • OSCON (Amsterdam, October 26-28): CFP closes Mat 11th
  • Full Stack Toronto (Toronto, November 14-15): CFP closes October 9th
