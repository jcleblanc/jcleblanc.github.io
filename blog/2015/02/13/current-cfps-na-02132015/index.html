Conference Calls for Proposals in North America (02/13)

| Comments

We have more conferences accepting proposals in North America. This list has a range of language and specialty conferences, and one of the big ones below is Grace Hopper.

I’ve sorted the list based on CFP close date, to make it easier to figure out which one to submit to first:

  • EmpireJS (New York, April 26-27): CFP closes February 15th
  • Self.conference (Detroit, May 29-30): CFP closes February 16th
  • CascadiaJS (Blaine WA, July 8-10): CFP closes March 15th
  • DevOps Days Toronto (Toronto, May 14-15): CFP closes February 28th
  • Front Porch (Dallas, Oct 7): CFP closes March 20th
  • Grace Hopper (Houston, Oct 14-16): CFP closes March 20th
  • CSS Dev Conf 2015 (Long Beach, October 26-28): CFP closes April 5th
  • Madison+ UX (Madison, July 10-11): CFP closes April 18th
  • Madison+ Ruby (Madison, August 21-22): CFP closes May 9th
  • AlterConf Chicago (Multiple Locations / Dates)
  • Twin Cities Code Camp (Minneapolis, April 25th)
